About the Conference

The First National Student SAIIE Conference brings together final-year Industrial Engineering students from all 26 South African universities to present their capstone design and research projects.

Participating institutions include the University of Pretoria, University of Johannesburg, University of the Witwatersrand, Tshwane University of Technology, Stellenbosch University and all other national institutions offering Industrial Engineering.

Purpose of the Event

  • Showcase top final-year Industrial Engineering projects
  • Connect students with consulting & engineering employers
  • Create a national IE talent platform
  • Strengthen SAIIE student chapters
  • Award outstanding national student projects
